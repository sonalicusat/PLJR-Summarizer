About Us



The PLJR Summarizer App aims to provide a short and precise summary description of the various judgment passed in the different courts of the country. This summarization could reduce the time and the efforts required by the lawyers who go through every judgment to find relevant details for their case. The Web App applies the number of an algorithm to arrive upon the judgment summary. This algorithm is provided to the user in form of a list of options in which the user can choose the desired option as per his requirement. Based on this algorithm decisions are arrived upon and a summary is presented to the user.
